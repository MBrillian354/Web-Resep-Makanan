Delicious Healthy Home-made Salad Recipe For Dormitory Students

Ingredients:

  • 150 gr pineapple, cut into cubes
  • 100 gr papaya, cut into cubes
  • 150 gr melon, cut into cubes
  • 100 gr grapes

Ingredients for yoghurt:

  • 125 gr strawberry yoghurt
  • 50 gr strawberry, chopped
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Make yoghurt sauce, mix all the sauce ingredients together, set aside.
  2. Arrange the fruit pieces in serving bowls, drizzle with yoghurt sauce when serving.
